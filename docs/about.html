About Our Server

Welcome to Project Nova, a fresh take on the classic Realm of the Mad God experience. We aim to provide a stable, fun, and engaging server environment for all players. Our focus is on community, balanced gameplay, and unique content.

Project Nova was founded with the vision of creating a unique and balanced RotMG experience that fosters a strong community. Our dedicated team is passionate about the game and committed to providing regular updates, events, and a secure environment for all players.

We believe in listening to our community and actively seek feedback to improve the server. Join us on this journey as we build the ultimate RotMG private server experience!

Key Features

Discover what makes Project Nova stand out:

  • Active & Friendly Staff: Our dedicated team is here to support you, answer questions, and maintain a positive environment.
  • Regular Updates: Expect frequent content updates, bug fixes, and quality-of-life improvements as we are a brand new server.
  • Discord Community Integration: Stay connected with fellow players and staff through our active Discord server.
  • All Classes & Abilities: All Realm of the Mad God classes. (including Samurai, Bard, Summoner, and Kensei)
  • Update Roadmap: Join our Discord for more information on future Updates.
  • Custom Items: Multiple Custom Items with LG, ST, UT, and EV tags.
  • Custom Set Bonuses: We are bringing back ST's with Set Bonuses.
  • Vanilla Experience: We are trying to make Project Nova as close to Realm of the Mad God as possible, while also making it enjoyable.
  • Donator Perks: We try to make our prices relatively cheap while also balancing their perks. Check the Shop for more info.
  • Monthly Events:: We are planning to have In-game Events every month.
